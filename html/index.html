PROJECTS

YMSL.US

YMSL STAT TRACKER

This project was created for the local Young Muslims chapter I partake in. They run their annual basketball league called YMSL and would resort to Excel sheets or pen & paper to track stats. And the eventual display of players' stats would be through a large Google Sheet.
I knew it could be better.

Using several technologies, I published a website for the players to access their stats 24/7 and introduce new players to the beauty of YMSL.

This site was made with HTML, CSS, Js, NodeJS, ExpressJS, Nginx, Docker, Google Sheets API, & Google Cloud Platform.

MUNICIPAL VOTER GENERATOR

I was reached out by an assistant of a local politician campaigning for a muncipal position. He told me their team were targeting the Muslim voters to gain their votes. But they were scanning a 30,000+ row call bank for Muslim voters.

This was a big problem.

With not too many lines of code, I developed a script that cross checks columns that the user wants from two CSV files and produces a resulting CSV file.

This site was made with HTML, CSS, Js, NodeJS, ExpressJS, Nginx, Docker, Google Sheets API, & Google Cloud Platform.

Ongoing Development

PRINCETON IT SERVICES CRM

As part of my most recent internship, I was assigned to a team of interns to migrate my company's CRM software, which was Microsoft Dynamics, to an in-house product. The goal of this project was to remove the bells and whistles a large product like Dynamics had and implement NLP for RFQ scanning.

Throughout the course of 3 months, we developed a website for PrincetonITS to track their customer relations, and more importantly, autofill new RFQ data with the use of AI. The benefit of this came in that it decreased the time spent by the procurement team of looking for data, reduced the error rate of the procurement team, and streamlined the whole process of opportunity creation & capitalization.

This site was made with ReactJs, CSS, Spring Boot, ChatGPT API, Nginx, MySQL, and AWS EC2 & RDS.
